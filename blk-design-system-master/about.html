Template Customisation

Method

A beautifully designed website can take months to put together, if done from scratch. Fortunately, over the past decade, developers and teams from all over the world have contributed massively to the developer community by creating amazing packages, templates, features, animations, designs, frameworks and so much more, for the purpose of making it easier for all of us.

This means, I don't have to reinvent the wheel. The wheel has already been created. In fact, in my case, the wheel, the tyre, the suspension, transmission, clutch, gears, you name it; has all been invented, packaged and made available so that I don't have to spend months and months on recreating that stuff. In many cases I don't even need to put it all together, it's already been done for me. I come in when it comes time to customise. To make the product unique and to make sure all the parts work together efficiently and effectively.

I take an already well designed website template (typically of your choice) and custom fit it to your business and brand. I replace all he dummy content with your business content. I change the layout of the template wherever and however you desire. I change the primary and secondary colors to fit your business brand. I change the fonts if desired and I add/adjust animations & effects to give your site a fantastic user experience.

You can search for free or paid templates online to find the perfect one to use as a foundation for your new business website. Simply go to Google (or any search engine) and search for 'Bootstrap templates for a business.' There are thousands of templates online to download. Alternatively, you can click on one of the links below to see a few sites that showcase some beautiful templates to download. Once you've found your one, you can either download it (or just copy the link) and send it to me when you're sure that's the one. I'll take that template along with your content (business information, pictures, product/service information etc). We'll then spend some time discussing exactly what you want and how you want it. Then I'll put it all together for you.

Responsive Design

Benefit

It'll be fully responsive! This means your website will look good on every/any screen size. The design and layout of your website content will adapt to each device's width and height so that everything fits appropriately on the screen.

Content remains readible and still looks professional, as it should, whether you're visiting the site from your mobile, your tablet or your computer.
