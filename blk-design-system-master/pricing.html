Pricing

Remember, pricing can vary depending on your specifications.

There are a few things to take into account. The biggest factor is the size of your desired website: how many web pages you need as well as number of sections per page.

Another thing to keep in mind is how basic or elaborate you'd like each section to be. Maybe 60% of your site will be straight-forward/basic and the other 40% will be rather elaborate thus taking more time to implement.

Lastly, desired effects, animations and overall user experience can also influence the cost, but only if the expectations begin to grow beyond what a typical business website would normally have.

If you scroll down a bit more to where you choose your website size, you can get an idea of the cost. Keep in mind, this is just a baseline. We will need to have a detailed discussion prefereably over a phone call or Zoom/Skype call to establish exactly what your specifications are, and then from there, an accurate quotation can be produced for you.
